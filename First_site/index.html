Jo Ellison

Community Artist

Jo Ellison is a community artist based in East London. Her practice includes facilitating workshops, creating large public art pieces with communities and her own painting practice.

  • Facilitating Workshops
  • Creating Public Art
  • Painting

Facilitating Workshops

She sees art as something everyone should have opportunity to engage with and enjoy, beliving that everyone has the capacity to be creative. Her practice includes facilitating a variety of visual creative workshops with people of all ages and abilities. Through these workshops she sees people grow in confidence, develop their own voice and build new relationships.

Creating Public Art

Jo often facilitates workshops which develop public art object. Primarily working in 2D these are often paintings or mosaics. These works aim to give local people ownership over their neighbourhoods and environment, supporting the development of a collaborative voice and highlighing the joy and hope of people within the space. These works have included creation of a mosaic for Shadwell Gardens Estate and a mural for Fern Street Settlement.

Painting

Additionally, Jo has her own painting practice, working on large, mixed media pieces which explore emotional wellbeing in relation to external events and decision making. Often her paintings explore these themes using storytelling, colour and texture, looking at moments of reflection and indecision.
